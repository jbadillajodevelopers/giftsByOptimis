About us

Nosotros

Gifts by Optimis is a company specialized in the design and production of souvenirs. We started operations 11 years ago in Costa Rica, with a small catalog of hats. And now we have operations in five countries and more than 20 product lines, being market leaders in: key rings, magnets, hats and costume jewelery.
Gifs by Optimis is led by Luis Fernández. He is a renowned Peruvian entrepreneur who in 1999 received in his native Peru the award for "Business Creativity", awarded by the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences (UPC) in conjunction with the newspaper with the largest circulation in Peru called El Comercio, but it was in 2003 that he took Costa Rica as his new home and 3 years later he started a leading company of memories for travelers.
Our company bases its success on excellent teamwork, where: we design, produce, import and distribute the best souvenir in the market. We currently have our own commercial operations and in alliance with the main souvenir distributors in 5 countries:
  • Gifts by Optimis, S.A. in Costa Rica
  • Britt Shop for: Costa Rica, Curacao, Antigua and Barbuda
  • Optimis R.D. in Punta Cana, Dominican Republic.
  • Cuy Arts, S.A. in Peru
  • Alvidart, S.A., in Madrid, Spain
  • Optimis Miami, LLC in the United States for: Florida and the Caribbean

In 2018 we introduce a new product line "Super Heroes of Costa Rica", based on 4 characters that motivate the care of the environment.
We invite you to learn more about our products and news through our social networks.
